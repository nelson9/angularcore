Who we are

How we work

  • We are a small company that takes very seriously what we do.
  • We love languages and we love teaching and enjoy seeing how our students learn and progress.
  • We customize and tailor every single student and group to their necessities and adapt throughout the course whenever is needed and that is the key to our success.
  • We understand that learning a language is not easy and much practice is needed and we make our best to provide you with that.
  • We constantly make the students know their level progression and provide them with different tools to carry on practicing all the different skills (oral, listening, grammar, reading) outside the classroom with podcasts, apps, books and plenty of other materials.
  • We follow the European Framework of languages and we also help in the preparation of official DELE exams

Our Teachers

All our teachers are qualified and experienced Spanish or Latin American natives who have a great understanding of the Spanish and English languages and know what’s the best way to teach every topic. We use our own materials and the best resources compiled throughout the years with many interactive and self-testing computer based resources and apps you can use when commuting. We are approachable, we do relaxed but professional lessons and we believe that lessons need to be enjoyable and fun in order to succeed. We understand the importance of cultural references to fully understand a new language and take it into account during the lessons.
